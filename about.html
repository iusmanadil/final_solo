About

Solo Tech LLC is a tech startup company based in the suburbs of Washington D.C. The company started based on the CEO’s own experiences and expertise of being a tech handyman. We saw a need to pioneer for more tech handymen that may not be industry certified but have strong expertise in implementing, fixing or troubleshooting various IoT devices, computers, hard drive swaps, memory upgrades etc. There’s a gamut of tech hardware and software in the market that requires a certain level of expertise to handle it. We are in an age where we want things done that require immediate attention and if we have time we’re willing to sort it out by viewing online resources like YouTube, Udemy, Lynda and forums however some things are simply over our heads and too much to take in so we rely on professional tech services until now; human capital can be expanded to locate specific people who have specific expertise and work or live in your vicinity. Solo Tech bridges the gap on getting expert services at reasonable costs with an option of finding qualified help above just certified help.

What's the difference

The difference between qualified and certiied? Price and availability. Certified techs are hired at higher market rate costs for the expertise they have and may not be readily available. Qualified techs are offered standard market rates to support consumer demand and usually have greater flexibility on their availability.

Whether certified or qualified expert, Solo Tech invests in the simple premise of human capital defined as the skills, knowledge, and experience possessed by an individual or population, viewed in terms of their value or cost to an organization or country.
